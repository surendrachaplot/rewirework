Our thesis

Slow isn't behind. It's by hand.

Most AI work right now is rushed. Half-built agents, generic dashboards, demos that don't survive contact with a real business. We work the other way — two people on the tools, building things that fit the way your operators already run.

We are not the largest consultancy. There are two of us. That is the point.

What we build

Six places we make work disappear.

Not chatbots. Not dashboards. Bespoke systems that plug into the tools you already use and quietly carry the repetitive load.

01

Customer communications

Enquiries, follow-ups, booking confirmations, reminders — handled in your voice, on your schedule. Not by you.

02

Scheduling and bookings

No back-and-forth to find a time. The system reads the room — your rules, your calendars, your busy seasons — and proposes the right slot.

03

Invoicing and payments

Invoices raised the moment they should be. Reminders sent without the awkward email. Cash that lands on time, more often.

04

Document processing

Contracts, due-diligence packs, intake forms, leases — read, extracted, filed, and routed without anyone touching a keyboard.

05

Reporting and insights

Performance reports built once, refreshed on their own. The Monday-morning spreadsheet ritual — quietly retired.

06

Lead qualification

Every inbound enquiry triaged before a human sees it. Time spent on the right people, not the wrong ones.

Who we work with

Small enough to know your business. Big enough to afford this.

We work best with UK businesses — 10 to 30 people, £100,000 a month or more in revenue — that are doing real work and stuck between scrappy and scaled. You can afford operators but not an in-house tech team. We are the in-house tech team you don't have.

How we engage — fixed scope plus retainer. We start with a defined piece of work and a price, then stay close once it is running. Not day rates.
How we work

Four steps. No drama.

01

Set the strategy

We map the work, find where time and money leak, and decide exactly what AI should be doing for you — and what it shouldn't.

02

Build the system

We build agents and automations, connected to the tools you already use and designed around how you already work. No rip-and-replace.

03

Hand it over

We train your team, write the documentation, and stay close while the system settles into the rhythm of your business.

04

Stay close

We stay on retainer. The system improves as your business grows — quietly, in the background.
